About Tidy

TIDY has created a high tech, home and office cleaning service.

TIDY offers $40 home cleanings. Based on the 80/20 rule (Pareto Principle), TIDYs are shorter but higher value. For $160, you can clean a 3 bedroom home with Homejoy/Handy once, or get 4 TIDYs. TIDY cleans smarter, prioritizing highly used rooms and rotating in less used areas over time.

TIDY doesn't just refer clients to cleaners, the TIDY platform improves cleaning. Every TIDY cleaner must pass TIDY certification (inspired by Ritz Carlton Training) and TIDY's app for cleaner uses proprietary technology to improve service over time.

Billing isn't done hourly, avoiding the "billable hours problem‚Äù which prevents innovation (e.g. lawyers). TIDY's technology enables hyper efficient scheduling, which is difficult to recreate.

Home cleaning is a $260B market and no company has >1%. Scaling is very hard, but smartphones are enabling change.

TIDY launched in June and grew its MRR >80% MoM (all plans are recurring).

Technology

TIDY has developed a proprietary internal software platform (named Samantha) to automate the management of this complicated business. First, TIDY gives customers a ton of flexibility and control over how they want their homes cleaned. Then, Samantha assigns, tracks, and communicates with Homekeepers via the TIDY iPhone app to maximize their success. The TIDY Front Desk acts as the control center, ensuring everything goes smoothly. Since launching the platform, TIDY has been able to handle dramatically more business, with only a few people.

The TIDY technology team includes Marcus Lopes (former head of Web Service at Red), Travis Anderson (Red, Brit+Co), Ivan Santos (Red), & Kevin Rafferty (SpotPay).
